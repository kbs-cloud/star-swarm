## New Features

- **Star-Swarm Gameplay**: Play a browser-based, turn-based tactical space strategy game to build, upgrade, and conquer planetary systems.
- **AI Skirmish Match**: Play single-player skirmishes against automated AI factions that expand, upgrade, and wage war.
- **Local Hotseat Multiplayer**: Play local multiplayer matches with friends.
- **Secure Lockdown Gates**: Shroud your tactical layouts and systems during hotseat player transitions to prevent other players from seeing your secret maps.
- **Interactive Starmap**: Navigate large galactic grids using smooth drag-to-pan and mouse-wheel zoom controls.
- **Fleet Control**: Dispatch fleets to target systems and recall them mid-flight if tactical situations change.
- **Production & Upgrades**: Queue starship construction and research system upgrades to maximize resource generation and defense.

## Changes

- **Port Configuration**: You can now launch the development server on a custom port using the `PORT` environment variable.
- **Ignore Drafts**: Excluded commit drafting helper files from version control tracking.
